Dish

Pinoy Creamy Carbonara
Ingredients:
  • 300 g spaghetti
  • 100 g bacon, cut into 1/2 inch slices
  • 15 g butter
  • 8 g garlic, finely chopped
  • 65 g button mushrooms, sliced or enoki mushroom
  • 70 g all-purpose flour
  • 38 g (1 pack) Knorr Cream of Mushroom dissolved in 3 cups water
  • Optional: 30 g spinach leaves picked
  • 240 g all purpose cream
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • grated Parmesan Cheese
Steps:
  1. Cook pasta according to package directions. Drain well and set aside.
  2. In a pan, cook the bacon until crispy and set aside.
  3. Using the same pan add the butter and saute the garlic until light brown. Add sliced mushrooms and all purpose flour. Mix well.
  4. Add dissolved Knorr Cream of Mushroom Soup and allow to simmer until slightly thick.
  5. Add the all purpose cream to thicken the sauce. You may add spinach leaves at this point. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Mix until all liquids are incorporated well. Do not let the mix boil as cream may separate and curdle. Turn off the heat and toss in the cooked pasta.
  6. Arrange the pasta in a plate then top with crispy bacon and Parmesan cheese.
